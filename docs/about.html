Himalaya College Open Source Club(HCOSC)

About Us

Himalaya College has formed a new open source club recently. There are some key members in important positions like President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer, Tech Lead and OutReach Lead assigned for their own important tasks.

The Himalaya College Open Source Club (HCOSC) is a student-driven community dedicated to promoting the values of open source, collaboration, and innovation. Our mission is to empower students to contribute to real-world open-source projects, learn modern development practices, and build solutions that make a positive impact on society.

At HCOSC, we believe in the power of sharing knowledge and working together. Whether you are a beginner eager to make your first pull request or an experienced developer looking to mentor others, this club offers a welcoming space for all.

What We Do:

  • Organize workshops, hackathons, and coding sessions.
  • Guide members in contributing to global and local open-source projects.
  • Collaborate on building open, impactful software tools.
  • Foster a culture of learning, problem-solving, and community-driven growth.
